Cibeles Insurance Corporation Celebrates 35 Years of Insurance Excellence

Sub title

Cibeles Insurance Corporation celebrated its 35th Anniversary and Christmas party last December 3, 2025 at the Century Seafood Restaurant in Century Park Hotel, Malate, Manila.

With the theme "Cibeles @35, Celebrating A 60 Year Legacy", foreign reinsurers, agents, brokers and other business partners, family, friends and employees graced this momentous event to celebrate 35 years of Cibeles Insurance Corporation, a journey built on trust, resilience, and insurance excellence.

Our President and CEO, Mr. Ruffy Kopio, shared the inspiring story behind Cibeles, honoring the legacy of his father, Rufino Ko Pio, whose vision and integrity laid the foundation for what we are today.

This milestone also recognized our loyal employees, our Founders Circle agents who started with Cibeles 35 years ago and continue to be part of our growth across generations, and our many current top producers who continue to spread Cibeles' culture of insurance excellence throughout the country today. As we remember the past and honor those who came before us, we look ahead with gratitude and commitment to continue delivering insurance excellence for many more years.
