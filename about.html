About Us: A Tradition of Service

Cibeles Insurance Corporation is one of the strongest and fastest growing non-life insurance companies in the Philippines. We built over 3 decades of service excellence on our reputation for prompt and efficient claims service, which has earned the respect and confidence of our agents and brokers in promoting our company's services to their clients.

From top management to the staff, service excellence is a way of life in all aspects of our operations. Top management have undergone, and continue to receive extensive local and foreign training to improve their knowledge, skills, and servicing capabilities in non-life insurance. We have a lean but highly efficient and results oriented staff that possess an urgency for action and service. From efficient claims servicing to providing prompt and highly competitive quotations to all your underwriting inquiries, we are guided by the desire to exceed our lofty standards of service, and be the insurer of choice among our agents, brokers, and policy holders.

It is this tradition of service that has made us one of the strongest and most respected names in the Philippine insurance industry. Our history of financial strength and stability speaks for itself. Our strong annual growth in premium volume, net income, assets and market share are the strongest manifestations of the trust and confidence the insuring public places on a Cibeles insurance policy.

This synergistic combination of financial strength and service excellence affords our policy holders the peace of mind to sleep soundly every night, knowing that behind every Cibeles policy lies the commitment of a results and service oriented group of insurance professionals dedicated to providing you the world class security and protection every Cibeles policy holder deserves.

Cibeles Insurance Corporation

Consistent with our goal of providing the security and peace of mind a Cibeles policy holder deserves, we are proud to be associated with highly rated Standard & Poor's and AM Best reinsurers based in Switzerland, Germany, China, Japan, South Korea and India. Our results driven focus in underwriting, marketing, and claims servicing has earned the trust and confidence of our reinsurers to provide us the capacity to write multimillion peso policies at highly competitive terms and conditions, making us a well respected name in the Philippine insurance industry.
